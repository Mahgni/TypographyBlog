The world is a big place! Thousands and thousands of unique cultures, languages and outlooks make up planet Earth today, and with that massive scope comes a myriad of design philosophies and ideas.

Let's take a look at some of the typefaces that have managed to permeate universal design. These typefaces appear frequently across the globe and are therefore some of the most famous pieces of graphic design ever created... however, possibly also some of the most overlooked.
